
UML SEQUENCE DIAGRAM - ONLINE ORDER PROCESS

Actors/Objects:
Customer, OrderService, PaymentGateway, InventoryService

Flow:
1. Customer -> OrderService: placeOrder()
2. OrderService -> InventoryService: checkAvailability()
3. InventoryService -> OrderService: availabilityStatus()
4. OrderService -> PaymentGateway: processPayment()
5. PaymentGateway -> OrderService: paymentConfirmation()
6. OrderService -> Customer: orderConfirmation()
