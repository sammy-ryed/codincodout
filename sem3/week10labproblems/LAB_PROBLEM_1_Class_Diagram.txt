
UML CLASS DIAGRAM - LIBRARY MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

Classes:
1. Book
   - Attributes: title, author, ISBN
   - Methods: issueBook(), returnBook()

2. Member
   - Attributes: memberID, name
   - Methods: borrowBook(), returnBook()

3. Librarian
   - Attributes: employeeID, name
   - Methods: addBook(), removeBook(), manageMembers()

Relationships:
- Member 1 --- * Book  (Association: A member can borrow many books)
- Librarian --- Book (Aggregation: Librarian manages a collection of books)
