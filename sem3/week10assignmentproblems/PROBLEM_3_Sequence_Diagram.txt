
UML SEQUENCE DIAGRAM - ONLINE SHOPPING SYSTEM

Actors/Objects:
Customer, Cart, PaymentService, OrderService

Flow:
1. Customer -> Cart: addItem(productID)
2. Cart -> Customer: itemAddedConfirmation()
3. Customer -> PaymentService: makePayment(amount)
4. PaymentService -> Customer: paymentStatus(success)
5. Customer -> OrderService: confirmOrder()
6. OrderService -> Customer: orderConfirmation()
